About Us

Our Mission

At Porsche, our mission is to create extraordinary sports cars that inspire a passion for driving. We strive to push the boundaries of automotive engineering and design, delivering unparalleled performance, innovation, and driving pleasure to our customers.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading global luxury sports car manufacturer, celebrated for our unrivaled performance, timeless design, and engineering excellence. We envision a world where Porsche cars are synonymous with driving enjoyment and admired for their craftsmanship and technological advancements.

Our History

With a rich heritage dating back to 1948, Porsche has a remarkable history of automotive excellence. It all began when Ferdinand Porsche, a visionary engineer, founded the company and introduced the iconic Porsche 356. Since then, we have continued to innovate and create legendary sports cars that have left an indelible mark on the automotive industry. From the legendary 911 to the groundbreaking Taycan, each Porsche model represents the perfect balance of performance, luxury, and craftsmanship.

Evolution of Leadership

Ferdinand Porsche

1950 - 1951

The visionary behind iconic Porsche models like the 911 and the Volkswagen Beetle. His engineering brilliance and passion for performance laid the foundation for Porsche's legendary heritage.

Wendelin Wiedeking

1993 - 2009

A mastermind of business and innovation, Wiedeking transformed Porsche into a global powerhouse. His relentless pursuit of excellence and business acumen drove Porsche to new heights, solidifying its position as a premier luxury sports car brand.

Oliver Blume

2015 - present

A driving force behind Porsche's modern success, Blume is a true leader. With his strategic vision, he spearheaded the development of groundbreaking electric models, ensuring Porsche's commitment to innovation and sustainability.
